After our jaunt in Jujuy, we traveled to Salta, the capital of the region Salta. There we picked up a car, and started our road trip! Our plan was to go from Salta⇒Cafayate⇒Cachi, and then back to Salta.

Salta to Cafayate

Thankfully this part of the trip was paved, and we got to see some cool touristy destinations on the way there! We visited La Garganta del Diablo, or the devil's throat, and El Sapo, or the toad

Devil's throat
The Toad

Cafayate to Cachi

Cafayate and Cachi are both valleys known for their wine. Lush valleys accompanied us on the road to Cachi. We sadly could not drink much wine since we were driving, but the wine we did get to try was delicious!

Vineyards

Cachi to Salta

Cachi to Salta brought us through Parque Nacional los Cardones, or Cactus National Park. I have never seen cacti as big as these. The road was largely unpaved, which meant we had to drive much slower than before. While the trip took us a long time, we were glad to take it slow so we could appreciate the great views!
